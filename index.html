About myself

I was born in Monza, a town in the northen part of Italy. I got a B.Sc. in Statistics at the University of Milano Bicocca in 2012. I moved to Padova, to join the M.Sc. in Statistics program, in the University of Padova, finished in 2015. From 2015 to 2019 I was enrolled in a PhD program in statistics, at the University of Milano-Bicocca, with a visiting period in the 2017/2018 at Trinity College of Dublin.

Since 2019 I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Milano-Bicocca, on the project Models for nonstandard data with high complexity, working on novel modelling and computational tools in the Bayesian nonparametric environment. For more details, see my CV .
